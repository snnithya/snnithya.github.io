Hi! My name is Nithya Shikarpur. I am a computer scientist and musician. I work as a research assistant at the Digital Audio Processing lab, IIT Bombay with Dr. Preeti Rao. My projects mostly revolve around the computational analysis of Hindustani music. I enjoy thinking about ways to make music (especially Indian classical music) more relatable and interactive for diverse audiences with the help of technology.

I finished my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from BITS Pilani. Before this I worked on machine learning projects for malware detection at McAfee. I also am a student of Hindustani classical music. In my spare time, I explore new genres of music, play with my neighbour’s dog - Appu, and make fun little animations on my laptop.

The portfolio section of this website is a log of all my scientific and artistic endeavours. It is divided into research, technical projects, music and dance as listed on the right. Feel free to explore whichever you like!
