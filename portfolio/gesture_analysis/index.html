Gesture Analysis of Hindustani Singers - Work in Progress

By Nithya Shikarpur 15 November 2021

This work is being done with Dr. Preeti Rao at the Digital Audio Processing Lab, IIT Bombay.

In an ongoing projects with Dr. Martin Clayton, Durham University, we extend our analysis to multimodal inputs. We aim to study the relationship between the hand gestures performed and the melodic content sung by a singer in the context of Hindustani vocal music. Apart from the expected new insights, this could potentially be used for raga detection and singer identification.
